PrioritizeRoutes is a Laravel 5.1 and up package that allows you to assign priority to routes.

Prioritizing routes can be very usefull when you want to ensure a catchall route is always matched last, allowing other routes that also match this catchall route to match first. More info in the Documentation.

PrioritizeRoutes is maintained by CupOfTea696.
